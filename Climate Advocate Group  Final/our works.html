Our Programmers

Climate Advocate Group - Empowering Rwandans in Climate Action The Climate Advocate Group, in collaboration with Eco Ed Connection, launched a campaign to educate and engage Rwandans on climate change. The initiative included educational sessions for over 700 students in Phase 1 and community meetings with more than 70 participants in Nyarugenge during Phase 2. The focus was on climate adaptation, water management, and sustainable practices. Key objectives were to enhance climate change awareness, teach adaptation methods, and empower community action. Challenges included resource limitations and sustaining engagement. Recommendations involve seeking funding, improving community involvement, and strengthening education efforts.

Urban Farming Initiatives

We support sustainable urban agriculture by training urban farmers, developing community gardens, and encouraging local food production. These initiatives help improve food security, reduce food miles, and promote environmentally friendly farming practices in urban settings.

Waste Management and Recycling Our clean-up drives, recycling campaigns, and educational outreach focus on improving waste management practices. We aim to reduce waste generation, increase recycling rates, and enhance community awareness about effective waste handling.
