Main Objective

Our primary goal is to empower communities and individuals to combat climate change through education, innovation, and advocacy.

Mission

To drive climate action in Rwanda by empowering communities and individuals through targeted education, innovative solutions, and grassroots advocacy.

Vision

A climate-resilient Rwanda where every community is actively engaged in sustainability efforts and empowered to lead in the fight against climate change.

Who we are

The Climate Advocate Group, founded by university students in
the 21st century, is dedicated to addressing climate change through grassroots engagement, education,
and innovative solutions. We aim to empower communities, especially the youth, to become climate
leaders and advocates for sustainable development.
